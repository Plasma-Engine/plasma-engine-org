## Severity Levels

- Sev 1: Full outage
- Sev 2: Partial degradation
- Sev 3: Minor impact

## Steps

- Identify alert and severity
- Assign incident commander and scribe
- Mitigate impact; communicate status
- Investigate root cause
- Record timeline and actions
